Personal Data Protection Notice พ.ร.บ. คุ้มครองข้อมูลส่วนบุคคล พ.ศ. 2562

The SCBM Alumni Association, Faculty of Science, Mahidol University is committed to protecting your personal data under Thailand's Personal Data Protection Act B.E. 2562 (PDPA). Please read the notice below before proceeding.

Data Controller

The SCBM Alumni Association.

Personal Data We Collect

  • Identity: Full name, nickname, profile photo
  • Contact: Phone number, email address
  • Academic: SCBM generation, modules studied, University of Sussex exchange
  • Professional: Status, employer, job title, industry, degrees
  • Online: LinkedIn, GitHub, portfolio, social media handles

Purpose of Collection

  • Maintaining the SCBM Alumni Database and Directory
  • Producing the SCBM Alumni Booklet
  • Organizing alumni events and homecoming activities
  • Facilitating alumni networking and mentorship
  • Communicating updates from the alumni association

Legal Basis & Retention

Collected on the basis of your explicit consent (Section 19, PDPA). Retained until purposes are fulfilled or consent is withdrawn. Data will not be sold or shared with third parties.

Your Rights Under PDPA

You have the right to access, rectify, delete, restrict, or object to processing, and to withdraw consent at any time. Contact the Data Controller above to exercise your rights.
